about

My name is Evan and I am a designer and photographer based in Lexington, Kentucky. I've been shooting and designing professionally for over five years. I specialize in product photography as well as real estate, and as a designer I specialize in print and web design, as well as branding and logo design.

In my rare free time, I like to cook, play my guitar, and watch sports. I am available for commercial shoots and design work, as well as collaborations.
